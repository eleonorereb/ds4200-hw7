Things I Am Interested In

One of my main interests is data, technology, and business. Data science allows organizations to make better decisions by analyzing patterns and trends in large datasets. For example, companies use data to understand customer preferences, improve marketing strategies, and choose the best price to sell their products. It is interesting how much insight can come from analyzing data carefully. Another interest of mine is digital products and user experience. Many of the applications and platforms people use every day rely heavily on data driven design. Often times, users dont actually know that thier data is being used. Product teams analyze how users interact with different features and then improve those features based on the data they collect. This process helps companies build products that are easier to use and sell. I am also interested in emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning - a class I'm yet to take at Northeastern! These technologies are changing many industries by making it possible to automate tasks and predict outcomes. Learning how these systems work and how they can be applied to real world problems is something I find very exciting. Overall, my interests revolve around understanding how technology/data can be used to solve problems and create a better world overall. As technology continues to evolve, data will continue to play a major role in shaping how businesses improve and how people interact with different products.

Some of my interests include:

  • Data science and analytics
  • Business strategy
  • Technology and digital products
  • Travel and exploring new cultures
  • Fitness and staying active
