Welcome to my website. My name is Eleonore and I am currently a student at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Business Administration. I am interested in how data can be used to understand behavior, improve business decisions, and design better digital products. Data science is a powerful field because it allows people to analyze large amounts of data and transform it into insights that help organizations make smarter choices. One of the reasons I enjoy studying data science is because it combines technical skills with problem solving. Businesses today rely heavily on data to understand trends, predict future outcomes, and make informed decisions. Learning how to analyze data and communicate insights clearly is becoming increasingly important across many industries. Data visualization, machine learning, and statistical analysis are all tools that allow data scientists to explore patterns and communicate findings. I also enjoy staying active and working out because it helps me maintain balance while managing school and other responsibilities.
